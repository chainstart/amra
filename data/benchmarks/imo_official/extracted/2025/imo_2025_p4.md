# IMO 2025 Problem 4

- Source: International Mathematical Olympiad official English PDF
- Source PDF: `data/benchmarks/imo_official/problems_pdf_en/IMO-2025-problems-eng.pdf`
- Day: 2
- Points: 7

A proper divisor of a positive integer N is a positive divisor of N other than N itself.

The infinite sequence a_1, a_2, ... consists of positive integers, each of which has at least three proper divisors. For each n >= 1, the integer a_(n+1) is the sum of the three largest proper divisors of a_n.

Determine all possible values of a_1.
